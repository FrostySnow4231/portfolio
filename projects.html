Projects

Christopher Wong

For this project my objective was to create a game that was able to function on a mobile app called App Inventor. My partner and I decided to do a mini golf game. The game itself was still very beta due to how the app functioned. Some features that we included were spikes that would send the player back to the beginning. We also included large obstacles that would impede the player. The map was fairly small because we had to keep in mind that most players would play on a mobile device. I was in charge of coding and some of the research that was done on how to make certain things look more aesthetic and also made some of our documentation.
